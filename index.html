Who is Bananapantz?

Bananapantz is a YouTuber/Animater that makes content for people of all ages. He posts mostly Minecraft content on his channel Bananapantz but also does other content like Super Mario Oddessy Speedruns. He also makes animations on his channel Bananapantz Animates There short little animations that he made himself but he commonly uses free online assets to make these animation videos.
