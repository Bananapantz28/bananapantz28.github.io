Summary

A small mod containing some guns and ammo, along with a villager profession

Mod Description

The content of this mod ranges from a stick filled with gunpowder to Flamethrowers and Nukes. It's yet another Gun Mod, though with some potentially stronger guns than most mods give you, for a pretty cheap price.
